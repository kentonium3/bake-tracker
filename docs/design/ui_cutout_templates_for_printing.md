## PRINTING TIPS

1. **Print on cardstock** for accordion sections and filter strips (more durable)
2. **Print on regular paper** for index card templates, then trace onto index cards
3. **Use colored paper** where specified for visual organization
4. **Print at 100% scale** - do not shrink to fit
5. **Cut carefully** along lines - precision matters for a professional look
